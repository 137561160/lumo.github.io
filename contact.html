Request a Quote

To receive an accurate quotation for your CNC machining project, please provide the following information:

  • Drawings or Models: 2D/3D CAD files (STEP, IGES, STL) or PDF drawings with dimensions
  • Material Requirements: Specify material type (e.g., Aluminum 6061, Stainless Steel 304, etc.)
  • Quantity: Estimated annual quantity or batch size
  • Tolerances: Required dimensional and geometric tolerances
  • Surface Finish: Desired surface roughness or finishing requirements
  • Special Requirements: Heat treatment, plating, coating, or other post-processing needs

Once we receive your information, our engineering team will review your requirements and provide a detailed quotation within 24 hours.
