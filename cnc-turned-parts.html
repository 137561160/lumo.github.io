High-Quality CNC Turned Components

At PartCNC, we specialize in manufacturing precision CNC turned parts for industries including automotive, medical, aerospace, and electronics. Our advanced CNC turning centers deliver exceptional accuracy, surface finishes, and repeatability for cylindrical and rotational components.

Precision CNC Turning

High-accuracy turning services for cylindrical parts with excellent surface finishes. Capable of handling various materials from aluminum to hardened steel with tight tolerances.

Tolerance: ±0.01mm

Swiss CNC Turning

Specialized Swiss-type turning for long, slender components and complex geometries. Ideal for high-precision small parts with excellent concentricity.

Tolerance: ±0.005mm

Multi-spindle Turning

Efficient production of high-volume turned parts with synchronized machining operations. Reduces production time and costs for large quantity orders.

Volume: 1000+ pcs
