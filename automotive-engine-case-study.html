Automotive Engine Component

Case Study: Precision manufacturing for a global automotive leader

Project Overview

Detailed look at our work with a global TOP3 automotive brand

Project Summary

A global TOP3 automotive brand required complex engine components with tight tolerances of ±0.005mm and challenging material (Inconel 718). These components were critical to engine performance and required exceptional precision and quality.

Industry Automotive
Tolerance ±0.005mm
Quantity 10,000+ parts

Challenge

The global automotive brand required complex engine components with tight tolerances of ±0.005mm and challenging material (Inconel 718). The components were critical to engine performance and required exceptional precision and quality.

Specific challenges included:

  • Complex geometries with tight tolerances
  • Difficult-to-machine Inconel 718 material
  • High volume production requirements (10,000+ parts)
  • Stringent quality standards with minimal defect allowance
  • Competitive delivery timeline

Solution

We utilized our advanced 5-axis machining centers with specialized tooling and custom fixtures to achieve the required precision for these complex components.

Our approach included:

  • Advanced CAM programming for optimal tool paths
  • Custom fixtures designed specifically for this component
  • Specialized cutting tools for Inconel 718 machining
  • In-process inspection to ensure dimensional accuracy
  • Dedicated production team with extensive experience in automotive components

Results

We successfully delivered over 10,000 components with 99.97% quality compliance, exceeding the client's expectations for both quality and delivery timeline.

Key outcomes:

  • 99.97% quality compliance rate
  • 20% faster delivery than competition
  • Became preferred supplier for additional components
  • Client reported improved engine performance metrics
  • Zero warranty claims on delivered components
